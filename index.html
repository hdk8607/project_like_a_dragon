December, 1988. Japan's Bubble era is in full effect. Young citizens are partying, businesses are booming, and underground crime has never been more active. Especially when rumors of an empty lot worth a billion yen surface, two factions of yakuza try to race to seize the plot of land. In this action-adventure beat-em-up game, you play as both Kazuma Kiryu, a yakuza fighting in Kamurocho, Japan, and Goro Majima, an ex-yakuza trapped in the town of Sotenbori, Osaka, who in the end, both dive into the underbelly of the crime world, decides the fate of the empty lot, and become future legends.
