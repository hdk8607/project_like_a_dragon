Kazuma Kiryu

A low-ranking member of the Dojima Family, Kazuma Kiryu is the first playable protagonist in the game.He works for his bosses, Keiji Shibusawa, Daisaku Kuza, and Hiroki Awano, who are liutenants to the main head of the Dojima Family, Sohei Dojima. However, after roughing up a man in an empty lot, Kiryu is now framed for murder of that man, and will go out of his way to do anything to prove his innocence. However, this investigation is thrown to the sidelines, as he discovers another player in the game...
