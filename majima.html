Goro Majima

In Sotenbori, Osaka, the Grand cabaret is one of the main well-known attractions of the town. And the floor manager of the Grand is the second playable protagonist of the game, Goro Majima. While on the outside he is witty and known for being "Lord of the Night", he is secretly in the cage of the town he currently resides in. Formerly yakuza of the Omi Alliance, a different yakuza faction and enemy of the Tojo Clan, he tried to defy orders, and in return, spent years in a solitary hole, tortured and now only having one eye. When he gets an offer to join back, in exchange for taking someone's life, he accepts, and slowly realizes how big this hit really is...
