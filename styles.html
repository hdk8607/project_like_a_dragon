Brawler Style

The brawler style is the first style you get as Kiryu. This style makes use of your fists to the fullest, and makes you "punch like a two-ton gorilla".

Thug Style

The thug style is the first style you get as Majima. This style makes sure you hit the enemy quickly and hard, just like people would expect from the Lord of the Night

Rush Style

Rush is the second style you unlock as Kiryu. While not as hard-hitting, the rush style makes up for it with speed, with dodges so fast you could be the next punchout artist.

Breaker Style

Breaker is the thrid style you unlock for Majima. This style not only values dodging, but also makes sure your break dance moves can be weaponized.

Beast Style

Beast is the third style you unlock as Kiryu. Unlile any other style, this one heavily relies on guarding with you body, withstanding even knives and bullets, and making sure your enemies can't feel their arms.

Slugger Style

Slugger is the second style you unlock as Majima. Unlike any other style, this one guarentees you always have a baseball bat, and makes you use it like a pair of nunchucks. It also enhances other weapon moves, I guess.
